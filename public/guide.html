Fitness-Aware Meal Ordering
Built on Swiggy MCP

FitOrder is a fitness-aware meal ordering agent built on Swiggy's MCP platform. Set your macro targets, log what you've eaten, and let the agent query Swiggy Food and Instamart MCP to return meals ranked by macro fit — with one-tap ordering.

The Problem

The problem with ordering healthy

People tracking fitness goals spend significant mental energy every meal cross-referencing apps, doing math on what they've eaten, and hunting through Swiggy menus hoping something fits. No delivery product today understands why you're eating — not just what you want to order.

What it does

What FitOrder does

Everything built around a single loop — remaining macros in, right meal out.

🎯
Macro-aware meal ranking
Every meal scored by how well it fills your remaining protein, carb, and fat gap — not just keyword search. Meals are ranked, not listed.
📐
TDEE calculator
Enter height, weight, age, and activity level. Mifflin-St Jeor BMR × multiplier → goal-adjusted macro targets set automatically. No spreadsheet needed.
📅
7-day meal planner
Full week of Swiggy orders planned around your macro targets. Shows weekly cost, avg protein per day, and per-day macro breakdown.
💰
Budget tracker
Protein-per-rupee efficiency score from your Swiggy order history. Best and worst value meals. Spend by restaurant. Macro compliance over time.
👤
Coach mode
Coach sets macro targets, shares a link. Client opens it with pre-loaded targets. They order within the plan. Coach sees compliance. No extra app needed.
💬
Conversational agent
Ask about swaps, veg-only options, budget constraints, or reorder a past meal. Full natural language reasoning powered by Claude API.
